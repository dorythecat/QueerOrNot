Queer or Not?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Queer or Not?

Queer or Not is an online social game, where you will get paired with a random person, and you will have to guess wether they are queer or not.

How do I play?

To play, simply click on the "Play" button on the main page. You will be paired with a random player, and you can start guessing their queerness.

Is it free to play?

Yes, Queer or Not is completely free to play. There are no hidden costs or in-game purchases.

What was the inspiration behind this game?

The game was inspired by the desire to create a fun and engaging way to explore and discuss queer identities in a light-hearted manner. It takes heavy inspiration from "Human or Not", a similar game where you are paired with either a human or an AI, and have to guess which one they are.
